Who am I?

A riddle is a statement or question or phrase having a double or veiled meaning, put forth as a puzzle to be solved. Solving riddles is a great way to increase productivy.

Read more about riddles here.

Top benefits of solving Riddles

  • Enhanced Memory
  • Accelerated ingenuity
  • Improves Reading Comprehension
  • Expands Vocabulary
  • Introduces Intellectual Humor

Below is one riddle for you to solve.

When I am visible to you, you cannot see me, but when I am invisible, you long to see me. I am plenty with someone patient, but all the more scarce with a hasty one. I am greater than all, but still in the control of those who value my existence. Who am I?
